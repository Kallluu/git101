A Little Introduction About Us

Disaster Prediction with Disaster Management

Our project aims to combine technology and humanitarian response to effectively predict natural disasters and manage their aftermath. Using data-driven models and real-time reporting systems, we empower authorities and the public to respond quickly and efficiently, minimizing loss and ensuring timely relief distribution.

  • AI-powered prediction of floods, earthquakes, and other disasters
  • Real-time alerts and interactive dashboards for authorities
  • Relief fund request and approval workflow for affected individuals
  • Efficient camp management and volunteer allotment system
